The Countdown

  1. "Pumpkin" Pretzels
    2. - dip most of an oreo in orange-colored chocolate
    - once hardened, dip the top of oreo in green chocolate
  2. Mummy Brownies
    3. - bake brownies
    - drizzle white chocolate on the top (to look like bandages)
    - add sugar eyes
  3. Spider Cookies
    4. - bake a shortbread cookie
    - stick a mini reeses peanutbutter cup upside down on the cookie (This will be the body of the spider)
    - use melted chocolate to make “legs” from the peanutbutter cup to the cookie
    - add sugar eyes
  4. Skeleton Oreo Pops
    5. - skewer an oreo through the center filling
    - dip in white chocolate and let harden
    - use black frosting to draw on eyes and a spooky smile
  5. Graveyard Pudding
    6. - fill a cup up with chocolate pudding
    - crush up oreos and put on surface
    - write "R.I.P" on a Milano cookie and stick it in the pudding
